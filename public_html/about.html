WELCOME TO INDIANOLA TRAILS

About

Indianola-only trails include Jerry Kelly and McVay, which are both under 3 miles in length. Near Indianola, Lake Ahquabi State Park contains a loop trail for all skill levels. The Summerset Trail is the longest trail in Indianola that connects to Carlisle. This website was created by Masumo Mwenyi & Kaitlyn Kottlowski.
