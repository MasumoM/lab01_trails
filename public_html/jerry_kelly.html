Jerry Kelly Trail

The Jerry Kelly Trail is almost 3 miles long. It starts at the trailhead of the Summerset Trail on E. Euclid Ave. The trail is named after the longest surving mayor of Indianola. It connects near by residents to schools, parks, and the other Indianola trails. The trail passes through Buxton Park at the northern end of Simpson College's campus.

TRAIL FACTS

  • Part of the trail is off-road
  • Passes through Memorial Park
  • Concrete surface
